Frequently Asked Questions

How does the "Be Your Own Bank" concept work?

Our program allows you to build cash value within your policy that you can borrow against at competitive rates. You maintain control over your money while it continues to grow, regardless of whether you have outstanding loans.

What are the tax advantages of this program?

Your policy's cash value grows tax-deferred, and loans taken against the policy are tax-free. Additionally, death benefits are generally paid to beneficiaries income tax-free.

How quickly can I access my money?

Once your policy has accumulated cash value, you can access your funds within 7-10 business days through our streamlined loan process.

Is there a minimum investment required?

We offer flexible premium options starting at $200 monthly, with the ability to adjust contributions based on your financial situation.

What happens to my policy if the market crashes?

Your policy's cash value is guaranteed and protected from market downturns, ensuring your principal and accumulated growth remain secure regardless of market conditions.
